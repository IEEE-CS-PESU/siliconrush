Silicon rush

The IEEE Computer Society is planning to conduct a hackathon in the month of September. Students will be working on themes that solve real-world problems that are spread across domains such as-

  • AI/ML
  • System Core
  • IoT
  • Cloud Computing
  • Blockchain
  • Software Dev etc

At the end of the 24- hour hackathon, teams will be deploying their app and presenting it to the judges.
